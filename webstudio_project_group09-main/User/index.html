Polaroid’s Comeback: Preserving and Displaying Instant Film

Photographic Materials

This article shows how to preserve and display Polaroid photos during the renaissance of instant film. Handle the film carefully while it is developing (no shaking, no folding, no cutting), keep the protective white border, and store in a cool, dry environment away from light and humidity. When displaying, use UV-filtered glass or acrylic and a shadow box or float mounting to protect the photos and emphasize the unique beauty of the instant film border.
